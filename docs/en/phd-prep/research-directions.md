# Research Direction Exploration

Analyzing and comparing different research directions for Ph.D. preparation.

## Evaluation Dimensions

- **Personal Interest** - Genuine interest in the topic
- **Skill Match** - Alignment with existing strengths
- **Development Prospects** - Academic and industry outlook
- **Competition Level** - Application difficulty
- **Advisor Resources** - Number of quality advisors in this field
