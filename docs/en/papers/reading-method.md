# Paper Reading Methodology

How to efficiently read AI papers?

## Three-Level Reading

### Level 1: Quick Scan (15 minutes)

Goal: Determine if the paper is worth deep reading

- Read title, abstract, conclusion
- Look at figures and tables
- Skim experimental results

### Level 2: Deep Understanding (2-4 hours)

Goal: Understand the paper's core contribution and methodology

- Read introduction thoroughly to understand problem background
- Carefully read methodology section to understand technical details
- Analyze if experimental design is sound
- Think about limitations and improvement directions

### Level 3: Critical Thinking (ongoing)

Goal: Form your own insights

- What problem does this paper solve?
- What is the core insight of the method?
- Do experiments adequately support conclusions?
- What can be improved?
- What inspiration does it provide for my research?
